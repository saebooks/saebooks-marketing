<section id="faq">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="sh">
      <span class="eyebrow brand">FAQ</span>
      <h2>The questions worth asking.</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="faq-wrap">
      <details class="faq" open>
        <summary>Is this really free?<span class="chev"><svg class="lucide" viewBox="0 0 24 24" style="width:18px;height:18px;"><polyline points="6 9 12 15 18 9"/></svg></span></summary>
        <p>Yes. Cashbook is hosted by us at no cost &mdash; no credit card, no time limit, no feature degradation. The codebase is AGPLv3, so even our paid tiers can be self-hosted by anyone who wants to. We make money on Business, Pro, and Enterprise hosted subscriptions and on enterprise engagements. Cashbook is the on-ramp.</p>
      </details>
      <details class="faq">
        <summary>Can I upgrade later without losing data?<span class="chev"><svg class="lucide" viewBox="0 0 24 24" style="width:18px;height:18px;"><polyline points="6 9 12 15 18 9"/></svg></span></summary>
        <p>Yes. Cashbook entries are real journal entries under the hood &mdash; the upgrade is a one-click flag flip. Every entry, every category, every dollar is preserved.</p>
      </details>
      <details class="faq">
        <summary>Can I downgrade from a paid tier back to Cashbook?<span class="chev"><svg class="lucide" viewBox="0 0 24 24" style="width:18px;height:18px;"><polyline points="6 9 12 15 18 9"/></svg></span></summary>
        <p>No. Cashbook is single-entry by design and full editions write data Cashbook can&rsquo;t represent &mdash; for example, accrual invoicing. The upgrade is one-way. Choose Cashbook if you genuinely need single-entry simplicity, not as a free trial.</p>
      </details>
      <details class="faq">
        <summary>What happens to my data if I leave?<span class="chev"><svg class="lucide" viewBox="0 0 24 24" style="width:18px;height:18px;"><polyline points="6 9 12 15 18 9"/></svg></span></summary>
        <p>Full export &mdash; CSV, JSON, OFX, and a Postgres dump. Your data lives in Postgres tables documented in our public OpenAPI. There&rsquo;s no proprietary format, no lock-in, no ransom for your books.</p>
      </details>
      <details class="faq">
        <summary>Do I have to do BAS?<span class="chev"><svg class="lucide" viewBox="0 0 24 24" style="width:18px;height:18px;"><polyline points="6 9 12 15 18 9"/></svg></span></summary>
        <p>Cashbook tracks GST collected and GST paid on each transaction so you have the numbers when BAS time comes. It does not lodge BAS &mdash; that&rsquo;s an Enterprise feature in early access. For now, copy the GST totals from the report into your existing BAS workflow (paper, agent, or MyGov).</p>
      </details>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

  (function() {
    var root = document.documentElement;
    var STORAGE_KEY = 'sae-books-theme';
    var mql = window.matchMedia('(prefers-color-scheme: dark)');

    function applyTheme(choice) {
      var resolved = (choice === 'auto') ? (mql.matches ? 'dark' : 'light') : choice;
      if (choice === 'auto') {
        root.removeAttribute('data-theme');
      } else {
        root.setAttribute('data-theme', choice);
      }
      root.classList.remove('light', 'dark');
      root.classList.add(resolved);
      document.querySelectorAll('.theme-switch button[data-theme-value]').forEach(function(b) {
        b.setAttribute('aria-pressed', String(b.dataset.themeValue === choice));
      });
    }

    function getStoredChoice() {
      var v = localStorage.getItem(STORAGE_KEY);
      return (v === 'light' || v === 'dark') ? v : 'auto';
    }

    applyTheme(getStoredChoice());

    document.querySelectorAll('.theme-switch button[data-theme-value]').forEach(function(btn) {
      btn.addEventListener('click', function() {
        var choice = btn.dataset.themeValue;
        if (choice === 'auto') localStorage.removeItem(STORAGE_KEY);
        else localStorage.setItem(STORAGE_KEY, choice);
        applyTheme(choice);
      });
    });

    if (mql.addEventListener) {
      mql.addEventListener('change', function() {
        if (getStoredChoice() === 'auto') applyTheme('auto');
      });
    }
  })();
</script>
